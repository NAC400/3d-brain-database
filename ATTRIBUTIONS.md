# MAPPED — Dataset Attributions & Legal Requirements

This product incorporates two third-party anatomical datasets. Both licenses
permit commercial use; attribution is mandatory. Requirements are documented
below so they can be embedded in the application's "About" page, any
published paper, and all derivative distributions.

---

## 1. Allen Human Reference Atlas – 3D, 2020

**License:** Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)
*(effective September 1, 2022)*

**What this means for MAPPED:**
CC BY 4.0 permits use in commercial products. You must include the citation
below wherever the data or its derivatives are presented or distributed.
No additional permission from the Allen Institute is required.

**Required citation (exact):**

> Song-Lin Ding, Joshua J. Royall, Susan M. Sunkin, Benjamin A.C. Facer,
> Phil Lesnar, Amy Bernard, Lydia Ng, Ed S. Lein (2020).
> *Allen Human Reference Atlas – 3D, 2020.*
> RRID: SCR_017764, Version 1.0.0.
> Allen Institute for Brain Science.
> Available from: https://atlas.brain-map.org/

**Full citation policy:** https://alleninstitute.org/citation-policy/

**Dataset download:** https://download.alleninstitute.org/informatics-archive/allen_human_reference_atlas_3d_2020/version_1/

**Where to display this in MAPPED:**
- App "About" / legal page
- Any academic paper or poster using the platform
- README of this repository (already referenced)

## 2. ICBM 152 Nonlinear Atlases (MNI Space, 2009)

**License:** Free for non-commercial and commercial use with citation requested
*(McConnell Brain Imaging Centre, McGill University / MNI)*

**What this means for MAPPED:**
The ICBM template is freely redistributable. The authors request citation of
the Fonov et al. 2011 paper in any academic context (papers, posters, theses).
Include it in the app's About page as good practice even for the commercial
product.

**Primary required citation:**

> VS Fonov, AC Evans, K Botteron, CR Almli, RC McKinstry, DL Collins,
> and the Brain Development Cooperative Group (BDCG).
> "Unbiased average age-appropriate atlases for pediatric studies."
> *NeuroImage*, Volume 54, Issue 1, January 2011.
> ISSN 1053-8119. DOI: 10.1016/j.neuroimage.2010.07.033

**Secondary citation (conference abstract, cite alongside primary if space allows):**

> VS Fonov, AC Evans, RC McKinstry, CR Almli, DL Collins.
> "Unbiased nonlinear average age-appropriate brain templates from birth to adulthood."
> *NeuroImage*, Volume 47, Supplement 1, July 2009, Page S102.
> Organization for Human Brain Mapping 2009 Annual Meeting.

**Dataset page:** https://nist.mni.mcgill.ca/icbm-152-nonlinear-atlases-2009/

**Where to display this in MAPPED:**
- App "About" / legal page
- Any academic publication using the platform or its spatial coordinates
